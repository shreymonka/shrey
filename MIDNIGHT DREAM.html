MIDNIGHT DREAM

This is not about a dream that you see in sleep or something like a fantasy dream it's about your real dream. The dream that you want to make it real one day.

  • So, most of the times when you are alone most probably at mid night this thing happen to you that you feel like if you can get back your childhood and re enjoy those days again with your family and friends. You feel like you have messed up in your life in past few years. And want to make those days better.
  • There will be many different feeling at that midnight. But then you think of your future that you want when you grew up.
  • Now that's the time when you really know what you want and what will make you happy. But this midnight dream last for only few hours that night and next day you will be back to your daily boring routine you will forget you had to work on some sought of dream that makes you happy.
  • Whenever I had to take some tough decisions I take in the midnight because at that time my mind knows what I want. What makes me happy .

So, at the end I am just trying to tell you that whenever you get a midnight dream that you want to make real just write it down as it is and read every single day so you can work on it and can get it true.
